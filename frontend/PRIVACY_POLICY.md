# Privacy Policy for DengueGuard

**Effective Date: May 13, 2026**

At DengueGuard, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
- **Account Data**: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and phone number to manage your account and provide personalized features.
- **Profile Picture**: You may choose to upload a profile picture. To do this, we require access to your device's camera or photo gallery.

### B. Health Data
- **Symptom Reports**: DengueGuard allows you to log symptoms and medical history. This data is used solely to provide machine learning-based dengue risk assessments and is stored securely on our servers.

### C. Permissions
- **Camera**: Used only when you choose to take a profile picture. We do not access your camera without your explicit action.
- **Storage**: Used to read/write profile pictures or export health reports.
- **Notifications**: Used to send you reminders for health checks and important health tips.
- **Biometric Data**: If enabled, we use your device's native biometric authentication (FaceID/Fingerprint) for secure login. We **never** store your actual biometric data; it is handled entirely by your device's secure enclave.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide AI-driven health risk assessments.
- To maintain and improve the application.
- To communicate with you regarding your account or health reminders.
- To ensure the security of your data.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We **do not sell** your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for medical research purposes, but this data will never identify you personally.

## 4. Security
We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 5. Your Rights
You can request to view, edit, or delete your personal data at any time through the "Privacy & Security" section in the app.
